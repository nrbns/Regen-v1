Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Data Collection

RegenBrowser collects zero data. We don't track you, we don't store your data, and we don't send anything to external servers.

2. Offline Operation

All AI processing happens locally on your device using Ollama. Your queries, documents, and voice commands never leave your computer.

3. Local Storage

Settings, bookmarks, and session data are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage and Tauri's secure storage.

4. Optional Online Features

If you choose to use online AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic), API keys are stored locally and requests go directly to those providers. We don't intercept or log these requests.

5. No Telemetry

RegenBrowser has zero telemetry, zero analytics, and zero tracking. We don't know how you use the browser, and we don't want to.

6. Open Source

All code is open source. You can audit it, modify it, and verify our privacy claims yourself.

7. Contact

Questions? Open an issue on GitHub.
