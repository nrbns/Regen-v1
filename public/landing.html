Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RegenBrowser

Is RegenBrowser really 100% offline?

Yes! Once you install Ollama and download the AI models, RegenBrowser works completely offline. Your data never leaves your device. No internet required for AI features.

Does it support Hindi voice commands?

Absolutely! WISPR supports both Hindi and English. Say "Nifty kharido 50" or "Research Tesla earnings" - both work perfectly. Language auto-detection included.

How much RAM does it use?

RegenBrowser is optimized for 4GB RAM devices. Tab hibernation automatically unloads inactive tabs. Typical usage: 500MB-1GB RAM (vs 2-3GB for Electron browsers).

Is it free forever?

Yes! RegenBrowser is 100% free and open-source under MIT License. No premium features, no subscriptions, no ads. Forever free.

Can I use it for trading?

RegenBrowser includes Trade Mode with real-time market data, AI signals, and voice trading. However, you'll need to connect your own broker API (Zerodha, etc.) for actual order execution.

What about privacy?

Zero tracking, zero telemetry, zero ads. All data stored locally. AI runs on your device via Ollama. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I contribute?

We're open-source! Star us on GitHub, submit PRs, report bugs, or suggest features. See CONTRIBUTING.md for guidelines.
